Paper:A GAN Model With Self-attention Mechanism To Generate Multi-instruments Symbolic Music

We propose a new GAN model with self-attention mechanism, DMB-GAN, which can extract more temporal features of music to generate multi-instruments music stably.
First, we propose a GAN-based music generation model with self-attention mechanism, which enables the model to extract not only spatial features but also temporal features, to generate more consistent and natural single-track music.
Secondly, we propose a dual generative adversarial architecture with multi-branches to generate multi-instruments music with harmonic structure among all tracks.
Finally, we introduce switchable normalization to stabilize network training to improve generated quality of multi-instruments symbolic music. .
